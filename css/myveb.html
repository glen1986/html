NO bake Creamy Biscoff Cake with strawberries

This This amazing cake is one of my best- love dessert, the cake so easy that even no need a bake. one piece of this cake is ever enough :)

Ingredients:
  • 800 grams 15% Sour cream
  • 4 package of biscuits
  • 6 teaspoons of cane sugar
  • resh strawberries

Sour cream layer:

In the bowl mix together the sour cream and cane sugar.

Biscuit layer:

arrange biscuit in the bottom of the square pattern in a single layer. For the corners,cut a biscuit in half so they fit snugly together. On top of the biscuit layer cream mixture and spread it over evenly. On top of the cream, add another layer of biscuits. You shoude have six layers 3 of biscuits and 3 of cream, the top being cream. For decoration,add fresh strawberries. cover with plastic wrap and place it in the refigerator for night. Enjoy!
